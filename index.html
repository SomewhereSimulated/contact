My Passion

In 1984, I started my lifelong journey with flight simulators using my Commodore 64 and Flight Simulator II. Since then, I took my passion as a flight simulation hobbyist, and made a career of it for almost 25 years. I began my professional career as Editor-At-Large for a flight simulation based magazine, MicroWINGS in the late 1990s. In 2002, I moved on to work for Flight1 Software for 18 years. In 2021, I co-founded my own flight simulation software company, Adventum Simulations.

My Skills.

Microsoft Flight Simulator Developer

I am an add-on software product developer for Microsoft Flight Simulator. I develop, along with a long-time friend, engaging and immersive addons for Microsoft Flight Simulator for PC and XBOX X|S platforms.

Web Developer

Besides the Adventum Simulations company website, I have created numerous websites for clients and for personal use. I am always trying to expand my knowledge and skills to perfect my craft. I am currently taking online classes for Full Stack Web Development.
